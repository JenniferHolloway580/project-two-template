About
Luce is a Italian restuarant that makes food to stay or go. The restaurant sits inside a little corner store. We also do catering. Our kitchen is run by Mr. Wesley Berger. See you soon!
John Taboada and Giovanna Parolari founder of Luce is best known for Navarre and Angel Face. Navarre serves 50+ wines by the glass and has food based in Italian, French and Spanish origin. Angel Face is a charming bar spot located on NE 28th Ave. Portland that serves French food.
